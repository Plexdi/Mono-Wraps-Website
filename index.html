Our Story

CEO OF
SHAWARMAS.

Mono Wraps started with one mission: to make the finest shawarmas and shish kebabs in north London. On Holloway Road, opposite the tube station, we've been doing exactly that.

Everything is made fresh. The salad is cut daily. The meat goes straight from the grill into your wrap. There are no shortcuts here — just good ingredients, proper technique, and a genuine love for what we do.

We serve chicken, lamb, kofte, and veggie options across our menu — as well as house-made hummus, baklava, and Turkish sodas. Whether you're eating in or ordering delivery, every wrap gets the same attention.

Vegan and veggie options available. We accommodate dietary requirements — just ask.
